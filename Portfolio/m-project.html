Graduation project

Cottage project, made in AutoCAD

Graduation work - COTTAGE facility is intended for short stays in the summer months. It consists of one residential units with a total area of ​​45.9m2 , of which 30m2 falls on the main floor, and 15.9 m2 to the gallery. The total height of the building is 7.65 meters. The ceiling height of the main floor is 2.50 meters, and 3.20 meters at the highest point of the gallery. The building is located on an overgrown plot forest. The plot extends down the slope, and the slope of the terrain is 25 degrees. Orientation the building is north-south, and the height of the building from the ground is 0.80m on the north side and 4.0m on the south side.
